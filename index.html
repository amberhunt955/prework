Hi, my name is Amber Hunt. Welcome to my webpage!

Introduction

For most of my adult life, I have been living nomadically as a backpacker. I just moved home to Ohio in September 2022 from New Zealand, where I had been living on a temporary work visa since December 2019. When I first arrived in the New Zealand, I had no idea that my one year visa would be extended 4 times due to the impending global pandemic. Getting "stuck" in New Zealand gave me the opportunity to work and travel in the country for nearly three years!

3 favorite things about NZ:

  • Hiking
  • Laid back culture
  • Sunshine

What surprised me about living in New Zealand?

I was surprised by how much I enjoyed the birds! New Zealand has some pretty special native birds. My favorite are Piwakawaka, Kea, and Weka, but there are so many more. I love that you can almost always hear the birds singing, even when you are in the middle of Auckland, the largest city in New Zealand.

How was I able to live and work in New Zealand?

If you or someone you know is between the ages of 18 and 30, has a US passport, and proof of $4200 NZD to help cover arrival costs, it is actually not too difficult to get the temporary visa I was on - a Working Holiday Visa. You can find more information on the New Zealand Immigration Website.
