Now, back to my favorite job in New Zeland - calf rearing on a dairy farm

I liked this job because it involved a lot of learning and a lot of problem solving. I didn't grow up on a farm so I had to learn how to handle animals, drive various farm vehicles, and more. Every day was different! I was always having to figure out what the most efficient way to go about my day would be.
